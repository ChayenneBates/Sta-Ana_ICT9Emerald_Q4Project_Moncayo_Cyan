Lunkashi Morticio Okapi


A demon who has a kind soul and stands out from all the demons in hell. After being casted out from hell and his family, he is captured by a secret organization called the SCP Foundation.

He is a Puppeteer demon who is known to use his tail to control humans, he is a cannibal though picky when eating.

Even if he is a demon, he cannot be killed by religious practices, not much known on why he can't die other than being burnt by holy water, he can't truly die by violence or anything.
